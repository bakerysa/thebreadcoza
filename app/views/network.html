Our Creative Partners

Beanstalk

With a portfolio of events including Balkanology, Jozi Craft Beer Fest, and Flamjangled Tea Party, it’s little surprise brands like Converse, Puma, and Louis Vuitton call on The Beanstalk when they need to make an event, a success.

Carl Ascroft

An architectural sensibility that offers up practical solutions and an aesthetic sensitivity that makes sure they’re beautiful. It’s Carl’s keen eye that has seen much of the recent revival and development in Braamfontein.

Daniel Ting Chong

His distinctive illustration style and design eye is turning Daniel into one of the country’s most recognised creatives. It’s also the thing that has earned him commissions from brands such as Nike, The New York Times, and Red Bull.

Radio

Radio is a design driven production company housing a diverse team of creative talent specialising in animation and illustration. Founded in 2011, with studios situated in London and Cape Town, our goal is to offer design based solutions for clients big and small.

Dean Westmore

Cape Town based Art Director turned spatial designer and fabricator, Dean has created spaces for the likes of Anatomy and many local eyewear brands. He also makes flatpack furniture in his spare time.

Eitan Stern

Eitan is Cape Town based founder of Legalese, a creative legal agency which has redesigned legal services to suit the creative sector, start-ups and growing businesses, by making the services affordable, accessible and user-friendly.

Frypan Mfula

Frypan is one of the most recognised names on the streets of Jozi. Not only did he start RHCT Online, an online store that stocks the best of local up-and-coming brands, he has also helped brands like Levi’s and Puma gain a cultural foothold in Jozi.

Johnny Kotze

One part man, one part musician and one part illustration prodigy. Johnny’s artwork has been featured around the world, while his commercial work has helped build brands like Mercedes-Benz, Virgin Mobile, Havaianas.

Kalashnikovv

Murray and Matt have been heavily involved in the South African fringe art scene for several years and run the hugely successful Kalashnikovv gallery in Braamfontein. Our go-to guys for anything art.

Kitsi Sebati

There’s a good reason Kitsi offers a fresh perspective on global trends and fashion culture. It’s because she travelled the globe to get it. From New York to Paris, to Italy where she gained her Masters in Trend Forecasting while employed at Pitti Uomo.

Lazi Mathebula

Lazi’s (or Greii Spaces) unique and unmistakably-local style has been creating waves for the last few years, recently affording him the opportunity to work with brands like Nike, Red-Bull, Str.Crd, and more.

NinjaBreadBoy

A long-time obsession with skateboarding inspires the graphic work of Ninjabreadboy. His line-based style of illustration reflects his passion for graffiti, tattooing and the skateboarding sub-culture.

Chad Goddard

As a copywriter by day and a co-owner of FATHER Coffee by any other time he has, Chad has established himself as a writer and creative who understands branding and positioning better than most.

We-Are-Awesome Film

If it’s a piece of local video content that got your attention, there’s a good chance WAA Film were behind it. In the last few years they’ve shot, cut, and produced for bands and brands from Little Dragon, and The Drums to Absolut, adidas, The Fader, and ABSA.

Success Maake

Success can either be found cruising around the CBD on his bike, working energetically behind his laptop, or effortlessly blending in with the various groups that frequent Jozi’s coolest cultural spots. Always hungry for a challenge and his design work speaks for itself.

Thesis

Having started Thesis, a lifestyle store, run cru, and events collective in the heart of Soweto, Wandile and Gale are admired by many in their capacity as true custodians and connoisseurs of culture.

Ali Maumela

Ali is continuously seeking the new. She revels in constant change and is an enthusiast that finds meaning in all forms. When she's not daydreaming about food, she's actively putting her creativity to good use and exploring different outlets.

Stilo Magolide

As one of South Africa's most dynamic rappers and style icons, Stilo's influence has grown massively in a very short time. Hailing from the south of JHB and being idolized by the youth of RSA means working with him always provides unique insights and even more unique performances.

Battalion

Battalion undoubtedly run some of the best venues and bar setups in South Africa. With experience that spans many years and multiple ventures the chances are high that you've experienced one of their setups before.

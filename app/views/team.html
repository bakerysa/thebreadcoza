This Is Us

ANTHEA POULOS

Most people know her as Anthea Knows Best, and there’s a good reason for that. With the best part of a decade of on-the-ground experience as Senior Researcher and Strategist at Instant Grass, she’s the go-to-girl when it comes to logic and local insight.

HANNAH SHONE

A recent graduate of Vega Cape Town, Hannah spent several weeks at BBDO Hong Kong before moving back to hometown JHB to join The Bread team permanently. She has a varied but solid design-style and is experienced in dealing with youth culture and lifestyle brands.

NICHOLAS CHRISTOWITZ

Though known for his work as a graphic designer and brand specialist, Nick is more than just the pentool behind the visual identities for some brands you’re definitely aware of. He’s also a co-founder of Father Coffee, a lover of Johannesburg, and a fan of design in every form.

KATLEGO MAPONYANE

Hailing from an event background, as both a DJ and an event producer, Katlego has developed an understanding of what it takes to make things happen. As a Jozi kid he has a keen knowledge of the various markets at play within the city and country as a whole.

ANDREW BERRY

The brains, beard, and bouffant behind premier youth culture website, we-are-awesome.com. Andrew is also an accomplished content creator, digital strategist, and marketer. In fact, though hard to believe for an export of the Mother City, the only thing Andrew isn’t doing, is nothing.

SEKH-FEI DE LACY

Growing up around photographers and advertisers, Sekh-Fei developed a natural capacity for content-creation. From video to photography he understands how to communicate in-line with a brand’s strategy and in a way that speaks to people. Sekh-Fei took up a permanent position at The Bread after spending a year in New York.

DYLAN MEADON

Dylan hails from years of experience as a UX designer and front-end developer. He knows why people use digital products the way they do, and how they interact with technology that is constantly at their fingertips. It is due to our mutual appreciation for the flux of the tech industry that Bakery was formed.

BYRON POLLEY

Fresh out of a Computer Science degree, this passionate tech and code fanatic is the newest member of The Bread and Bakery team. Capable of anything from fixing the office printer, to developing feature-rich websites from scratch. In his spare time, Byron finds new ways of combining music and technology.
